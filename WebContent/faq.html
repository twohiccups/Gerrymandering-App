About/FAQ

What can I do here?

Our web app allows you to customize and/or witness congressional redistricting within the United States. Specifically, on here, you can test out redistricting for Massachussets, Ohio, and Oregon. The factors we've put in place help eliminate unfair gerrymandering.

What is Gerrymandering?

Gerrymandering is a technique of redistricting which enables political advantages for one party by manipulating district boundaries in specific ways. The two main techniques of gerrymandering include "cracking" and "packing".

  1. Cracking: diluting the voting power of the opposing party's supporters across many districts
  2. Packing: concentrating the opposing party's voting power in one district to reduce their voting power in other districts
